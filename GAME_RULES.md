## ğŸ² Strategy Tips

### 1. Avoid Playing High Cards Early
- Don't lead with Kings or Aces unless necessary
- Someone might cut and you'll pick up the pile!

### 2. Cut Strategically
- If someone plays an Ace or King, consider cutting if you can
- Make them pick up a large pile!

### 3. Empty Suits Quickly
- If you're down to 1-2 cards of a suit, try to play them
- Being empty in a suit gives you cutting power

### 4. Watch Other Players
- Track which suits are being exhausted
- Predict when someone might cut

### 5. Control the Lead
- Winning tricks lets you choose the next suit
- Lead with suits others might be weak in
